====PISM at AGU 2014====

{{:news:fm300x200.jpg?200 |}}

At the [[http://fallmeeting.agu.org/2014/|AGU Fall Meeting 2014]], PISM simulations and results were featured in a number of posters and oral presentations.


===posters===

  * Gudfinna Adalgeirsdottir and others, [[https://agu.confex.com/agu/fm14/meetingapp.cgi#Paper/21827|Role of Model Initialisation for Projections of 21st Century Greenland Ice Sheet Mass Loss]]
  * Andy Aschwanden and Mark Fahnestock, [[https://agu.confex.com/agu/fm14/meetingapp.cgi#Paper/9080|Quantifying improvements in ice sheet modeling (Flow Is Plastic, It's Fantastic)]]
  * Robert Fischer and others, [[https://agu.confex.com/agu/fm14/meetingapp.cgi#Paper/9083|Requirements for Energy-Conserving Two-Way Coupling of a GCM and Ice Model]]
  * Shfaqat Khan and others, [[https://agu.confex.com/agu/fm14/meetingapp.cgi#Paper/12379|Modelled and observed present-day state of the Jakobshavn Isbrae, west Greenland]]
  * Ioana Muresan and others, [[https://agu.confex.com/agu/fm14/meetingapp.cgi#Paper/7768|Impact of External Forcing on Glacier Dynamics at Jakobshavn Isbrae during 1840-2012]]
  * Lisbeth Nielsen and Christine Hvidberg, [[https://agu.confex.com/agu/fm14/meetingapp.cgi#Paper/22564|Sensitivity of the Greenland ice sheet evolution and flow on basal conditions--a parameter study with the PISM model]]
  * Shuting Yang and others, [[https://agu.confex.com/agu/fm14/meetingapp.cgi#Paper/22294|Modelling the Climate--Greenland Ice Sheet Interaction in the Coupled Ice-sheet/Climate Model EC-EARTH--PISM]]
  * Florian Ziemen and others, [[https://agu.confex.com/agu/fm14/meetingapp.cgi#Paper/19619|Flow Modeling of Juneau Icefield]]
