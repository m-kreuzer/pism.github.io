====== 1st European PISM Workshop in Hamburg, Germany, 21-22 May 2012 ======

The European PISM (Parallel Ice Sheet Model) workshop will be held
in Hamburg, Germany, from Monday, the May 21, 2012 (starting at noon),
to Tuesday, May 22.  Workshop information and registration is available at
